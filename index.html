site_title by smallspark

Extension to Radiant CMS that allows you to include a site title in your templates.

The site title should be set in a configuration setting with a key of ‘site.title’. Also allows for the option to output the site title along with the page title, as would be displayed in the tag of a HTML page. Example outputs Your Site Title With page title outputs Your Page Title » Your Site Title Custom separator outputs Your Page Title | Your Site Title

Install

script/extension install site_title
